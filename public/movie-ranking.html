The Best Movies According to Jacob

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Iron Man

This is my favorite superhero movie and I was fascinated by the character Robert Downey Jr.

Casino Royale

This is one of my favorite action movies and it's also my favorite 007 series movie.

God of Gamblers

This movie was starred by Chow Yun Fat who was one of my favorite Hong Kong movie actors. I have watched this movie for hundreds of times because the story was really interesting.

Vivi the Cinderalla

This is my favorite romance movie which tells a love story between a boy called Jacob and a girl called Vivi. Vivi was born in a middle-class family in the U.S. and Jacob was born in the Royal family in Hainan Kingdom. Vivi had to try so hard to become a family member of Huang's. In the end, Vivi became the princess and had a happy life with Jacob.
